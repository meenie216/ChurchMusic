# Church Music Assistant Instructions

## Song Selection

When asked to suggest songs for a service:

1. **Check the rotation file** (`Saturday Song Rotation.md`) for last play dates
2. **Suggest 5 eligible songs**, then recommend a **set of 3** from those 5
3. **Match songs thematically** to the readings or sermon topic provided

## Rotation Rules

- Songs should not be played more often than **once a month** (approximately 4 weeks between plays)
- **Exception for new songs:** A newly introduced song is played again after a one-week gap (i.e. first play, skip one Saturday, second play), then drops into the normal monthly rotation. Check `New Songs.md` under `## Introduced` for songs with an unchecked box — these are still in the two-play window and can be suggested the following week regardless of the one-month rule.

## New Songs.md

This file serves two purposes:

- **## Potentials** — albums and songs being evaluated for introduction
- **## Introduced** — a log of introduced songs. Format: `* [ ] Song Name — introduced DD Mon YYYY`. Once the second play has happened, mark as `[x]`.

## Updating the Rotation File

When song picks are confirmed:
- Move the current "This week" songs to the top of "Previously"
- Add the new songs to "This week" with their play dates
- For songs already in the file, add the new date and remove the old standalone entry (don't duplicate)
- For brand new songs, add a fresh entry with the publication year in parentheses if known, e.g. `* Song Name (2018)`
